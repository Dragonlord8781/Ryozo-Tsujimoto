Ryozo Tsujimoto

"Describe the Monster Hunter community in three words!" - "Hunter," "Friends", "Gratitude"

Summary

Ryozo Tsujimoto is a producer at Capcom, mainly focusing on the Monster Hunter series, as well has been the series's figurehead of sorts. But has helped out with other games as game supervisor, producer, or game crew members.

Notable games: Monster Hunter(2007), Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, Resident Evil: Outbreak, Monster Hunter: World, Monster Hunter: Rise, Monster Hunter: Wilds

Timeline/Facts

  • Born October 18, 1973 in Osaka, Japan
  • Began Working at Capcom in April 1996
  • Start with planner of acrade games, working games like Battle Circuit or Auto Modellista
  • Eventually began working on console games such as the first Monster Hunter game, Monster Hunter(2004)
  • He has been the producer of the Monster Hunter series since Monster Hunter Freedom 2 in 2007
  • Head of Consumer Games Development Division 2 2020-present
  • Executive Corporate Officer Apr. 2022-present
  • Director and Deputy in charge of Development Divisions of Capcom Jun. 2022-present
  • Son of Capcom founder and CEO, Kenzo Tsujimoto
  • Younger brother of Presiden and COO Haruhiro Tsujimoto
